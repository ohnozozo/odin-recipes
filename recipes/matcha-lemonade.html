Matcha Lemonade

Fancy a matcha but don't fancy consuming a lot of milk? A matcha lemonade is the perfect pick-me-up treat for you! With a splash of added lemon, this treat is full of anti-oxidants and goodness!

    Ingredients Required
  • One Half Teaspoon of Ceremonial Grade Matcha
  • Cold Water
  • Hot or Boiling Water - 80c is preferred
  • 250ml of Lemonade
  • OPTIONAL: Lemon to garnish
